Health maps

Disease-burden choropleths built end-to-end by FFL workflows on Facetwork, from open data. US coverage is strong (CDC); worldwide, only some causes have current open country-level data, so the world map shows the non-communicable-disease burden (which includes cancer, cardiovascular disease & diabetes).

  • US disease burden by state — death rates (cancer/stroke/diabetes/Alzheimer's/COVID) + flu activity (CDC)
  • US disease prevalence by county — adult prevalence of cancer, diabetes & stroke (CDC PLACES)
  • World disease burden by country — diabetes + NCD mortality + COVID-19 / HIV / measles, by country (WHO / World Bank)
  • US respiratory-virus hospitalizations over time — COVID-19 / flu / RSV new admissions per 100k by state, with a month slider (~5 yrs, CDC NHSN)
  • US hospital strain — bed occupancy over time — % of inpatient & ICU beds occupied, and the share held by each virus (CDC NHSN)
  • US respiratory ICU severity over time — share of hospitalized COVID / flu / RSV patients who are in intensive care (CDC NHSN)
  • US respiratory admissions — children vs adults — admission rates per 100k by age group; RSV & flu hit kids hardest (CDC NHSN)
  • US "tripledemic" — combined respiratory burden — COVID + flu + RSV admissions per 100k, winter over winter (CDC NHSN)

Published from Facetwork.
