Social Vulnerability Index choropleths for all 50 states + DC, by county. Click a state to open its map. Each county's SVI is the mean of six percentile-ranked indicators (poverty, unemployment, education, age 65+, no-vehicle, renter) — ranked within that state, so SVI percentiles are not comparable across states. The median poverty column is a raw rate and is nationally comparable. Data: US Census Bureau ACS 2023 + TIGER.
